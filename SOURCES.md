## Notes on sources

Episode citations follow the standard Season×Episode format used by Wikipedia, IMDb, and the major Friends fan archives. Where an item is established across the series rather than in a single episode (e.g. apartment layouts, recurring catchphrases, casting facts), that is noted in the source line.

A handful of questions reference recurring jokes that span multiple episodes (Janice's catchphrase, "How you doin'?", Smelly Cat, etc.). For those, the citation points to the *first* major appearance — the episode that established the joke — rather than every episode that revisits it.

If you spot an error, open an issue or PR on the repo.
